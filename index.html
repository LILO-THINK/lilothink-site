Intersection of Creativity,
Technology, and Science

At LILO.THINK, we work at the intersection of creativity, technology, and science. Our mission is to connect ideas from different fields and create innovative and inspiring solutions through radical imagination.

We believe that creativity is the ability to connect concepts, tools, situations, and experiences to generate groundbreaking ideas. We apply structured methodologies such as design thinking, design sprint, metadesign, and agile processes to stimulate and develop innovative solutions in various areas, including innovation, art, and science.

Our Journey

With a solid track record of working with major international companies such as Google, Udacity, and Nestlé, we have extensive experience in creating interactive corporate installations and delivering lectures at innovation, art, science, and technology events.

Our focus has always been on building bridges between these diverse fields through radical imagination and innovative methodologies such as design thinking, design sprint, metadesign, and agile processes.

These methodologies enable us to foster highly creative, unconventional ideas that break new ground, as demonstrated by our successful collaborations and impactful projects.

Innovation Space Consultancy

We offer consultancy for creating physical or digital spaces that promote inspiration and innovation. Leveraging radical imagination and methodologies such as design thinking and metadesign, our goal is to help our clients develop creative thinking and materialize their ideas into functional prototypes, especially in the context of maker spaces.

Innovation Projects

We develop customized projects that leverage artificial intelligence (AI) as a co-pilot for imagination. Utilizing structured approaches like design sprint and agile processes, AI expands the creative process, connecting technical and innovative approaches. We offer solutions for both the physical and digital worlds, always with a creativity-centered approach.
